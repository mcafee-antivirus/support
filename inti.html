Scheduled scans ensure your PC is protected from malware infections without manual intervention. They are designed to check your system thoroughly and automatically for viruses and other threats. To run a scheduled automatic scan using Systweak Antivirus follow the steps below:

  • 1.Launch Systweak Antivirus.
  • 2.Click the three stacked lines at the top left corner of the home screen.
  • 3.From the drop-down click Settings.
  • 4.Next, under Scan click Schedule.
  • 5.Here select, the type of scan you wish to perform, specify the time and day.
  • 6.Once done check the box next to Scan your computer at a scheduled time.
  • 7.Click Apply and that's all.

Now, Systweak Antivirus will run an automatic scan at the time and date you specified.
